The armadillo club

Rocky took Ivory to the Armadillo club where she is met with Tito the 3 banded armadillo, Rex the giant armadillo and Ranger the 9 banded armadillo. “WOW! That’s a lot of armadillos! And they all look so different too!” Said Ivory in pure awe. “Yes” Added Rocky “We all live here in the Amazon despite our differences!” Rocky then spoke to the other 3 armadillos: “Hey everyone! Meet Ivory! She is a screaming hairy armadillo and she needs our help!” Rex, the giant armadillo spoke up in his gruff voice “Ivory, where do screaming hairy armadillos live?” “¡Si!” Tito the 3 banded armadillo chimed in “I have never heard of the name screaming hairy before!”
