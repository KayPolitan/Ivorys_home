The 6-banded armadillo

The friendly frog went on and on about the wonders of the Amazon rainforest. From the plants, the Amazon river, and the animals! Then another type of armadillo, looking completely different from Ivory, walked up to them in confusion. “Excuse me miss lady…” he looked over at Ivory. “Are you an armadillo? Because you look like one.” “Yes I am!” Ivory said with confidence. “I’m a screaming hairy armadillo! My names Ivory. Are you an armadillo?”
