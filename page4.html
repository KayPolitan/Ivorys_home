The Friendly Frog

"As our armadillo friend looked for help, she came across a small pond with a big rock next to it. She got closer, for a drink then suddenly… Out came a green friendly frog! Ivory let out a screech to warn the frog to stay away but the friendly frog insisted. “Woah calm down little amigo calm down…You do not seem like you’re from around here! Are you lost?” said the friendly frog. “Yes I am lost in fact” said the little armadillo. “I knew it! You’re not the type of armadillo I normally see around here. Where do you live, mi amigo?” Asked the friendly frog."
